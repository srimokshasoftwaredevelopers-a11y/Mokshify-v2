Legal · Privacy

Privacy policy.

Effective 17 July 2026 · applies to mokshify.io

Written to describe what this website actually does - which is very little with your data, by design.

What we collect on this website

  • Nothing you type, automatically. This site has no account system, no tracking cookies, and no forms that submit to our servers - contact happens through your own email or WhatsApp client, under your control.
  • Standard server logs. Our hosting provider records the usual technical entries (IP address, user agent, pages requested) for security and reliability; retained briefly, used for nothing else.
  • Analytics, only if enabled. Our analytics code is inert unless a measurement tool is connected. If we enable one, this policy will name it and what it collects before it runs.

What you send us directly

When you email hello@mokshify.io or message us on WhatsApp, we receive what you send - your address or number, and the content of your message. We use it to reply and, if we work together, to run the engagement. We do not sell it, rent it, or add it to marketing lists you did not ask for. Ask us to delete a conversation and we will, unless a legal or contractual obligation requires keeping it.

Client project data

Data inside client engagements is governed by each engagement’s own agreement - which takes precedence over this page. Our standing defaults: your data stays in your systems and cloud accounts, access is least-privilege, and NDAs are available before any material changes hands. Our security practices apply throughout.

Third parties this site touches

Fonts load from Google Fonts (Google receives the standard request metadata that any resource load involves). Outbound links - client sites, WhatsApp - lead to services with their own policies.

Your choices & contact

Questions, or a deletion request: hello@mokshify.io. We reply within 24 hours, like everything else. Material changes to this policy will change the date at the top of this page.
