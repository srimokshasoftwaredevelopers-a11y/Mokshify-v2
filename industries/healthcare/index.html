Industries · Healthcare

Healthcare software with clinical seriousness.

Healthcare software carries a weight most software never feels: an ambiguous label or stale record has a human cost. We build for that weight - and we have a live diagnostics platform to show for it, not a slide about one.

What we build for healthcare

  • Diagnostics platforms - test catalogues, transparent pricing, booking and reporting flows. Live example: Medico Diagnostics, publishing 3,291+ tests with 4–6 hour reporting.
  • Patient-facing portals - appointment, records and results access designed for worried people on phones.
  • Operations systems - lab workflows, sample tracking, referral management; the software behind the counter.
  • AI with checkpoints - summarisation, triage and document extraction with human final say and audit trails, per our AI Transformation discipline.

The engineering posture

Healthcare work inherits our strictest defaults: the database schema treated as a promise to a patient, role-based access with audit logs on every privileged action, encryption in transit and at rest, and content precision reviewed like code. Compliance honesty is part of the posture: we build systems so that a future audit is a documentation exercise, and we state plainly which certifications we do and do not hold.
