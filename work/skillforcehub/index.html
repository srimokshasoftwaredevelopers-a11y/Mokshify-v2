Case study · Education · skillforcehub.com

SkillForceHub — careers, launched.

A Salesforce training institute in Warangal with a promise bigger than its city: industry-relevant skills, taught seriously, leading to real jobs. The platform had to carry that credibility online — courses, batches, outcomes and enrolment in one place.

  • Salesforcecareer tracks
  • Warangaltier-2 talent, global market
  • Onlinediscovery to enrolment

The problem

Tier-2 cities are full of capable people and short on credible bridges into tech careers. SkillForceHub teaches Salesforce — one of the most employable enterprise skills — but a training institute without a serious web presence loses students to louder, worse options. The site had to make quality legible.

Challenges

  • Credibility transfer. Curriculum, instructors and outcomes had to be presented with the same rigour the institute teaches with.
  • A mobile-first audience. Students research courses on phones between classes and shifts; the platform had to be fast and legible on modest devices.
  • Enrolment friction. From “interested” to “enrolled in a batch” in as few steps as possible, with human follow-up where it matters.

Architecture & stack

A fast, content-forward React platform on the standard Mokshify base: Node.js services, PostgreSQL for courses, batches and enquiries, Redis caching, Nginx at the gateway, releases through the tested, AI-reviewed GitHub Actions pipeline. Course pages are structured and crawlable — students search for exactly what this institute teaches, and the platform is built to be the answer.

Results

  • The institute’s full offering — tracks, batches, enrolment — lives online at skillforcehub.com.
  • Course discovery and enquiry work end-to-end on mobile.
  • The platform scales with the institute: new tracks and batches are data, not redesigns.

Lessons learned

For education businesses, the website is the first lecture — it teaches the student what standard to expect. Treating course content as structured data with real information density beat brochure-style pages on every measure that matters. That approach now informs our platform work for every credibility-driven business.
