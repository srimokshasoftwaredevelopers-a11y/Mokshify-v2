Services · Custom software

Software shaped like your business.

Off-the-shelf tools fit until they don’t: the workflow that makes you different is exactly the one no vendor ships. Mokshify builds internal platforms, operations systems and customer portals shaped to how your business actually runs - engineered to outlive us.

What we build under this heading

  • Internal platforms - the operations brain: approvals, scheduling, inventory, reporting, the spreadsheet-graveyard replacement.
  • Customer portals - self-service front doors on your existing operations, like the Medico Diagnostics catalogue.
  • Workflow automation - the repetitive 20% of operations, removed; often the fastest payback in software.
  • Integrations - the connective tissue between tools you already pay for, built on disciplined API engineering.
  • Rescues - systems that are fragile, opaque or abandoned: audit, stabilise, then improve, without downtime.

Why custom does not mean risky here

The dangers of custom software - lock-in, knowledge silos, rot - are process failures, and our process is built against them: boring technology, documentation that ships, tests that gate releases, and your ownership of everything from week one. The operations manual is public; hold us to it.

Common questions

What counts as custom software?

Systems off-the-shelf tools stopped fitting: internal platforms, dashboards, workflow tools, portals, integrations. If your team lives in spreadsheets that talk to nobody, that is usually the start.

Can you take over software someone else built?

Yes - audit first, stabilise, then staged improvement while it keeps serving users. See the patterns we rescue from.

How do you keep it from becoming legacy?

Boring tech, deployable documentation, release-gating tests, and your ownership of every repo - so the software outlives any vendor, including us.
